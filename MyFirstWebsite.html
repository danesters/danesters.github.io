Mario Bros.

Mario has long been a part of the gaming community catching the eyes of millions of players and even those who don't play can most likely tell you who he is. But where did he come from?


His History

Mario was created by Shigeru Miyamoto, a Japanese video game designer. While Miyamoto was creating Mario, he was also developing Donkey kong. During the process, Mario was unnamed in Donkey Kong in the Japanese release but was named Jumpman in the English release. How he got the name Mario is a bit amusing. Supposedly, during the beginning of spreading Donkey Kong to American audiences, Nintendo's warehouse owner got into a heated debate due to rent not being paid. In the end, the employees promised the rent and named Mario after him.


Mario's Buddies

Here are some characters you might recognize that are part of the Mario games and franchise :]

  • Wario

    • Wario is one of Mario's enemies and once was Mario's final boss to defeat. Wario has become a protagonist and makes cameos here and there.

  • Donkey Kong

    • Donkey Kong/DK is a gorilla and one of Mario's first enemies. He's appeared more and more and even has a branch of his own games.

  • Luigi

    • Luigi is well known, being Mario's brother and sidekick, throughout the Mushroom Kingdom. Luigi has increased in popularity and in 3 games ('Mario is Missing!','Luigi's Mansion' , and 'Luigi's Mansion:Dark Moon') he appears as a hero, instead of the Mario, to go and save his brother.


  • Princess Peach

    • Princess Peach is known as being the princess of Mushroom Kingdom and often the love interest of Mario. She's the damsel in distress being constantly targeted by Bowser and his gang.

  • Bowser

    • Bowser is known as the main antagonist in the Mario games. He constantly targets Princess Peach and tries to defeat Mario in order to take over the Mushroom Kingdom


  • Princess Daisy

    • Princess Daisy or Daisy, is the princess of Sarasaland, but is currently in Mushroom Kingdom. She has made reoccuring appearances within the Mario franchise and games. One of her traits is that she especially loves flowers hence her being represented by flowers.
